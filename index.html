These are the public git projects of the Chair of Lighting Technology of the Berlin Institute of Technology:


Texas Instruments DLP LightCrafter Framework:

communicate with the LightCrafter via MATLAB: TI-LightCrafter-MATLAB-Framework


Common Tools (to be filled with content soon...):

common tools needed in lighting technology for MATLAB: LightingTechnology-MATLAB-Tools


Tools for Measurement Devices (to be filled with content soon...):

MATLAB tools for measuring luminance images with a TechnoTeam LMK: TechnoTeam-LMK-MATLAB

tools for working with CS2000 spectroradiometer: KonicaMinolta-CS2000-MATLAB


Tools for Simulation (to be filled with content soon...):

easy simulation of road scenes with Radiance: Radiance-RoadLighting-Simulation-Framework


Tools for Evaluation (to be filled with content soon...):

evaluate Simulation / Measurements with MATLAB : VisibilityLevel-Evaluation-MATLAB
